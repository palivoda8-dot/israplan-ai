## Professional Knowledge Base (Planning in Israel)

### Statutory Framework
- **Planning and Building Law, 1965:** 
    - **Section 61:** Plan objectives regarding health, sanitation, and environment.
    - **Section 62A:** Local vs. District authority powers (critical for determining project scale).
    - **Section 63:** Authority to set provisions for nature/landscape preservation and open spaces.
    - **Section 145:** Basis for development permits and potential delays due to environmental nuisances.

### National Outline Plans (TAMAs)
- **TAMA 1:** The unified national plan. Sets technical standards for:
    - **Runoff Management (LID):** Retention, infiltration, and groundwater enrichment.
    - **Ecology:** Setbacks from forests (50m) and rivers/streams (20-50m), ecological corridors.
    - **Nuisance Prevention:** Noise, air quality, and waste management during construction/operation.
- **TAMA 35 & Change 1:** Strategic plan for development and preservation.
    - **Textures:** Urban, rural, coastal, protected, etc.
    - **Densification:** Shift towards mixed-use and public transport-oriented development.

### Technical Workflows
- **MAVAT (SV1):** Primary source for statutory documents and regulations.
- **GIS/Data:** Using `MAVAT_NAME` column in PLAN layers as the source of truth for land-use designations.
